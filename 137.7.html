137.7 is Sasha Myshkina's most ambitious project yet. Her feature-length directorial debut, 137.7 is an experimental surrealistic horror-thriller, shot, edited, scored, written and directed by Myshkina alone, and shot partially on her phone. Not a traditional film in any particular sense, what at first will appear to not have a plot and just seem to be unrelated clips and still will piece together into more of an experience than a film.

The film draws on many inspirations, notably, the work of David Lynch and his abstract surrealist masterpiece Inland Empire. It will also be similar in tone and stlye to her earlier two found-footage shorts, Everything and Nothing at the Same Time and Untitled.

137.7 is set to be released on the 15th of December, 2021, and production is already well underway.
